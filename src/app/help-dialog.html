People List Format

Create teams from a list of people. This tool was originally built to make teams from families attending a school ping-pong tournament, but can be used to make teams from any set of people. The simplest way to use this tool is to enter one person per line and Team Generator will create random teams based on the options you select.

Sometimes you may want more control over team creation. In particular, it is common to want certain people to be on different teams. With this tool you can controll that by putting those people on the same line separated by commas. Team generator will ensure people on the same line are put on different teams. For example, each line could represent a family whose members you want on different teams.

You can go further by adding one or more categories to each line. Categories are created using colon separators, and allow you to control how people in those categories are distributed across teams. For example, each family line could have categories for kids and for parents that the tool will evenly distribute accoss teams to avoid teams of all parents or kids.

Rather than famlies with parents and kids, you can use whatever categories you have. For example, each line could be a school class with "TeacherList: StudentList". You can also not add any categories or use more than two. Team Generator will always try to put the people on each line onto different teams and then either spread out people in the same category or group them together based on the options you select.
