People List Format

Create teams from a list of people. This tool was originally built to make teams from families attending a school ping-pong tournament, but can be used to make teams from any set of people. Each line is a group of people who you want to be on different teams if possible. Each line could be a family or any group of people who you want on different teams. Within each line there are two categories of people, like parents and kids that this tool can either spread out on different teams or put together.

The format of each line is
ParentList : KidList
Where ParentList and KidList are comma seperated names. For example, a line with two parents and one kid would be
Ava Peters, Eric Peters : Weston Peters
content_copy
The ParentList or KidList on each line can be empty. An example line with parents but no kids
Jack Manning, Kelly Manning :
content_copy
You may leave out the : seperator as a shortcut way to make each person on a line part of the KidList (the second category). An example line with only kids
Samantha Peake, Sister Peake
content_copy
Rather than famlies with parents and kids, you can use whatever categories you have. For example, each line could be a school class with "TeacherList: StudentList" or have just one category of people. Team Generator will always try to seperate the people on each line into different teams.
